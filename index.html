Our Story

We Are Aura Framing

Since 2022

Born from a love of love.

Aura Framing was founded with a single belief — every couple deserves a wedding captured in a way that perfectly reflects their unique love story. What began as a photography studio in Coimbatore has grown into a trusted name for weddings and creative shoots across Tamil Nadu.

Our passionate team brings together expertise in photography, videography, branding, and digital marketing — united by one purpose: to make your most important moments extraordinary in every way.

We don't just capture events. We frame memories, preserve emotions, and create timeless visual stories of the most beautiful chapters of your life.

"Every detail is a love letter to your story."

Common Questions

Package FAQs

What is the difference between Traditional and Candid photography? +
Traditional photography covers key posed moments — rituals, family portraits, and formal shots. Candid captures natural, unposed emotions and behind-the-scenes moments throughout the day.
When will I receive my album and video? +
Albums and edited videos are typically delivered within 4–6 weeks after the wedding. We keep you updated throughout the editing process.
Can the outdoor shoot be done before the wedding? +
Yes — the outdoor shoot in Package 02 can be done as a pre-wedding or post-wedding session, entirely at your preference and convenience.
Can I customise a package? +
Absolutely. All packages can be adjusted. Contact us and we'll create a tailored quote to match exactly what you need.
How do I confirm my booking? +
WhatsApp us at +91 77086 97816 or fill the booking form. We'll confirm your date and send you the next steps right away.
